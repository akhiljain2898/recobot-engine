Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary: We charge ₹15 only when your reconciliation has mismatches. If the Excel report failed to generate due to a technical error on our end, you are entitled to a full refund. No refunds are issued once the report has been successfully downloaded.

1. Our Pricing Model

easemyreco operates on a pay-per-reconciliation model:

  • Zero mismatches — the reconciliation report is provided free of charge
  • One or more mismatches — a flat fee of ₹15 (inclusive of all charges) is charged before the report can be downloaded

You are shown the full mismatch count before any payment is requested. You choose whether to proceed with the payment.

2. Refund Eligibility

Situation Refund
Excel report failed to generate due to a technical error on our side Yes — full refund
Payment was charged but the download link did not appear Yes — full refund
Report was successfully downloaded No refund
You uploaded incorrect files and got an unexpected result No refund
You changed your mind after payment No refund
Session expired before download (60-minute window) No refund — please re-run the reconciliation

3. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund, contact us within 48 hours of the payment with the following details:

  • The Razorpay payment ID from your payment confirmation email (starts with pay_)
  • The date and approximate time of the transaction
  • A brief description of the issue

Reach us at: admin@verifiedwork.co

We will review your request and respond within 72 hours. Approved refunds will be processed back to the original payment method within 5–7 business days, subject to Razorpay's processing timelines.

4. Duplicate Payments

If you were charged more than once for the same reconciliation due to a technical error, the duplicate charge will be refunded in full. Please contact us with both Razorpay payment IDs.

5. No Cancellation After Download

Because easemyreco delivers a digital report that is immediately accessible upon download, the service is considered fully delivered once the Excel file has been successfully downloaded. No cancellations or refunds are possible after this point.
